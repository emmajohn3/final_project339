Day 9

In the morning, we took a train to Dachau, and then a bus to the entrance of Dachau Concentration Camp. We walked through the museums, barracks, and grounds of the camp.

Once we headed back to Munich, we ate lunch at a Mexican restaurant, shopped a little bit, and sat down at a restaurant right by the Glockenspiel for desserts. We watched the Glockenspiel performance from the restaurant as the figures spun around while songs were played on bells.
